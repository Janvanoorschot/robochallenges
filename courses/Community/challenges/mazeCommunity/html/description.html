The Maze Runner 1

Robo has to automatically find the beacon in the maze.

One strategy is to always keep the wall to your right hand and follow it around corners and bends until you find the beacon.

We start with a simple maze, so you can quickly see if you are on the right track.

Use this strategy to let Robo automatically find the beacon in the maze. Use at least repeat(.){...} if(.){...}, and rightIsClear.
