Map Chest

General information

A collection of maps with which you can define your own challenges.

The maps are roughly sorted with respect to their content which is listed below.

No hints are available in this mode, but you can program in any of the currently available 27 languages!

Map sortDescription
Beacon mapsAll sorts of maps in which structure and/or marks can be used to find beacons
Line mapsAll sorts of maps with lines
Maze mapsSmaller and larger mazes
Move mapsAll sorts of maps in which you can move beacons around in a structured fashion
Open Spaces mapsSmaller and larger maps of open spaces
Places mapsVarious maps of places like the disco, or a castle
Random mapsSmaller and larger maps with beacons and/or dots randomly distributed
Spot mapsAll sorts of maps where spots can be used to guide the robot
