Walking a grid: random search

Solve the grid with Method 2: make a random decision at every intersection of white lines about where to go.

Let the robot search the grid in a random fashion to find the black spot.

Note: this may take a long time and Robo will not always find the black spot fast enough. That is no problem. Robo did only not run until the end, because this may take a very long time and we did not want to keep you waiting. Try again and/or make your program smarter (next exercise) to see if Robo finds the spot this time."
