Walking a Grid: algorithms
Two ways to attack this grid are:
  1. Cover the entire grid. If you walk on every white line, you will eventually find the black spot.
  2. Wander around randomly while looking for the black spot. Walk on the white line, and every time you get to a decision point (an intersection), make a random choice which way to go.
