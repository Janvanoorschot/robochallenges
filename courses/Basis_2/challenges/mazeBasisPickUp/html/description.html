Finding beacons in a maze

To let Robo find the beacon in a maze, we want him to always keep the wall to its right. When he has found the beacon, he is ready.

Remarkably enough you only have to tell Robo what to do in 4 situations. In the next exercises we will explore these situations one by one. Attention: the order in which you tell Robo about these situations is very important!

In this exercise we will explore two situations: (1) Robo has the beacon in front, and (2) the wall is to his right and there is nothing in front of him.

Complete the script to let the robot automatically find the beacon and pick it up.
