Robo's secret letter "A" (2)

To write more than just one secret letter, it is handy to let Robo always end his writing in the same way.

Adjust procedure secretA, such that Robo will stop right next to the square as shown in the image.

This way he will be ready and in a good position to write a new letter.

Now you know how to write the letter A in Robo's secret language!
