Robo finds a beacon all by himself!

Do you still remember that by randomly driving around Robo could find beacons like in the vacuum cleaner exercise? Robo went ahead one step (if he could) and would sometimes go left and sometimes go right depending on flipping a coin with flipCoin.

Create a procedure findBeacon to do it. Let Robo pick up the beacon once he finds it. Use flipCoin to decide which direction Robo goes.

Note: This can take Robo quite some time. Or very little time. If you're lucky ...
