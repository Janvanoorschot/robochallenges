Using flipCoin

Robo can flip a coin too! The command flipCoin does the same thing as when you would flip a real coin: in 50% of the cases you will find heads up, and in the other 50% you will find "tails" up.

It is not so, by the way, that head and tails take turns: it is perfectly possible that you will have a sequence of 5 times tails. It is only not very likely (3%).

Push the button a number of times to see if Robo turns right or left.
