What are robots good at?

Finding beacons automatically

A solution:
  • Let Robo just randomly move through the map until he finds the beacon ...
  • This is ok, because he never gets tired, can quickly do a lot of work, and never gets bored.

Finding beacons automatically

Is this a good solution?
  • Perhaps he will do too much work.
  • Perhaps it can be done faster.
  • But Robo does not mind as long as he finds the beacon(s).

Finding beacons automatically

How can this be done?
  • You must make sure that Robo eventually visits each and every place!
  • To do this you can flip a coin:
    • If head, go right
    • If tails, go left
