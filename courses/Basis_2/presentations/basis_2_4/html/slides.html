A procedure is like telling how to bake a cake
  • procedure bakeCake
    {
    • Mix(Butter, Sugar, Eggs, Flour)
    • Put it in the oven!
    }
A procedure is like telling how to greet in Chinese
  • procedure chineseHello
    {
    • Fold your hands and make a small bow, then
    • Stand upright and lower hands next to the body
    }
