What are barcodes?

  • A bar code consists of black lines and white lines.
  • These lines are read by a scanner
  • White lines are read as “1” and black lines as “0”.
  • These zeros and ones form a (binary) number.
    For example: 10001101001
  • 10001101001 translates to a common number.
    For example: 10001101001 --> 1129
